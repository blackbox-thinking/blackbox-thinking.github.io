Blackbox Thinking started as a technology consultancy when I got fed up of working freelance through an agency. I wanted to take more control of how and what direction my work took me.

Now that I've enjoyed learning and working in the tech sector for over two decades I have something I can give back. I wish to give people who want to learn tech skills a simple and exciting path to benefiting their own businesses, careers and hobbies. I needed a place that would reach a large audience. I needed to find people who genuinely wanted to learn mastery of technology.

That place is here. That person is you.
